Root-cause and fix the wlogout Shutdown/Reboot blank-screen hang (FIX-01) — diagnosis first (keyboard-vs-mouse test, journalctl/coredumpctl), then fix the class of bug by making the session-teardown actions uwsm-correct — and close the v1.0 tech-debt carry-over by listing rsync explicitly in install.sh PACMAN_PKGS (DEBT-01).

Output: A documented root-cause write-up, uwsm-correct session actions in wlogout/layout and powermenu.sh, rsync in PACMAN_PKGS — verified by 5 clean shutdown/reboot cycles and a container-gate rerun.
